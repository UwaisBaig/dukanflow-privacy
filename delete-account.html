Delete Your DukanFlow Account

Last updated: 26 June 2026

DukanFlow, developed by M. Uwais Baig, allows you to request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

How to Request Deletion

Step 1. Send an email to uwaisbaig50@gmail.com from the email address associated with your DukanFlow account, with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".
Step 2. In your email, please confirm: your account email address, and whether you are an account owner or staff member.
Step 3. We will process your request and confirm deletion by email within 14 days.

What Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your account email address and display name
  • Your authentication credentials

What May Be Retained

If you are a business owner account, requesting deletion will also remove your business record, including outlet configurations and outlet codes. Historical sales and stock entries created under your business may be retained in an anonymized or aggregated form for the legitimate business record-keeping purposes of any staff accounts still linked to your outlets, unless you specifically request full deletion of this historical data, in which case we will delete it within the same 14-day period, subject to any applicable legal or accounting record-retention obligations in your jurisdiction.

If you are a staff account, your personal account and login credentials will be deleted. Sales and stock entries you previously logged will remain attributed to the outlet's historical records for the business owner's continuity and audit purposes, since this data forms part of the business's own operational records, not solely your personal data. If you wish historical entries to be deleted entirely rather than retained as part of the business record, please state this explicitly in your deletion request, and we will accommodate this where it does not conflict with the business owner's legitimate record-keeping needs.
